[TaZmen Sniffer Protocol (TZSP)](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TZSP) is an encapsulation protocol used to wrap other protocols. It is commonly used to wrap 802.11 wireless packets to support Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS), wireless tracking, or other wireless applications.
